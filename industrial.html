Industrial Piping Systems
Benefits of Industrial Piping
  • Ideal for exposed and trenchless pipe installations
  • Withstands tough environments
  • Available in various diameters and colors
This product group by AGRU offers industrial users a wide range of pipes for transporting aggressive media, but also fittings, valves and special components which are all essential in this field of application. We offer materials such as PE, PP, PVDF and ECTFE to handle the variety of operating conditions in industrial applications. Besides material diversity, the individual pipe components give users the ability to build pipelines across a wide range of dimensions and flow rates, thus making these products a perfect choice for plant construction. Particularly in conveying aggressive media, and also acids, the robust properties of plastic piping systems guarantee operating safety for many years. The pipes, fittings, valves and special components for industrial applications are joined by means of proven welding methods such as butt, infrared, socket or E-socket welding. Accessories facilitate the installation and welding machines round off AGRU's comprehensive range. Materials such as PE (polyethylene) and PP (polypropylene) are extremely important in this area. High chemical resistance, the large temperature application range, and easy routing are just a few of the major benefits of these plastics. Applications range from cooling water pipes, pipe lines in plant construction, process pressure lines for transporting aggressive media through to pipelines for transporting contaminated wastewater, exhaust gas and exhaust airlines, and piping systems in apparatus and tank construction.

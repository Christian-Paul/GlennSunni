This product range includes a wide selection of premium PE 100 products for gas and water supplies and for wastewater disposal. Besides our PE pipes, our PE 100 fittings, valves and special components offer a long service life and freedom from maintenance, particularly in this field of application. All popular, positive joining technologies such as heating element butt welding, E-socket welding and socket welding can be used, thus ensuring flexible laying even in very individual conditions. Renovation of old piping is not just important in wastewater disposal today, but increasingly in water and gas supplies. Due to the increase in applications in this area, AGRULINE's great product diversity is hugely important. For trenchless installation or installation without sand embedding of pipes for gas, water and wastewater, our crack- and concentrated-load resistant Sureline pipes made of PE 100 RC, or SurePEX pipes, are an excellent solution. AGRU SureFIT pipes, which are laid using the close-fit method are the perfect choice for renovating legacy pipes without leaving an annular space. The cost intensity of the installation or renovation of supply networks poses massive requirements on the pipe material and/or the products themselves to guarantee a permanently leak-tight system for generations.

Agruair/Agrusan
Benefits of Agruair/Agrusan
  • Ideal for exposed and trenchless pipe installations
  • Withstands tough environments
  • Available in various diameters and colors
Agrusan
This system with fittings and pipes for compressed air is made of high quality PE 100 blue material. Besides a large number of fittings, which are available with or without metal threads, and corrosion resistant pipes, AGRUAIR also includes valves and installation accessories. These PE 100 products, which are produced on state-of-the-art machines, not only impress with great customer satisfaction in universal compressed air applications, but also guarantee operating safety for many years. What is particularly worthy of note is the system's resilience against compressor oils, which can cause problems for other materials. AGRUAIR piping systems are available in a dimension range of between 20 mm and 110 mm; they support compressed air supplies in many industrial applications. Such as in automotive, foodstuffs and mining.
